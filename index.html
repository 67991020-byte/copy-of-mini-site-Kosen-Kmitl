Welcome to KOSEN-KMITL

The KOSEN-KMITL institute is a collaboration between Thailand and Japan to specialized in producing high-quality engineers. Our curriculum focuses on practical engineering skills from an early age, fostering innovation and leadership.

Why Choose Us?

  • 5-Year Continuous Program (starting from age 15)
  • Japanese Standard Curriculum (Model Core Curriculum)
  • Hands-on Experience with Advanced Technology
  • Opportunities to work in Japan and with leading industries

Established to support the EEC (Eastern Economic Corridor), we aim to be the center of excellence in engineering education in ASEAN.
