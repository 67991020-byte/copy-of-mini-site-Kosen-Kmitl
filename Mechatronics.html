Diploma of Engineering in Mechatronics Engineering

A 5-year program focusing on the integration of mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. Students learn to design and maintain automated systems, robotics, and smart manufacturing technologies.

Program Highlights

  • Robotics: Design and control of robotic systems.
  • Automation: Industrial automation and PLC programming.
  • Control Systems: Precise control of mechanical systems.
  • Smart Manufacturing: Industry 4.0 technologies.
