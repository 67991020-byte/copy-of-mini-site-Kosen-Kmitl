Diploma of Engineering in Electrical and Electronics Engineering

A 5-year program that builds expertise in electrical circuits, power systems, and electronic devices. Students gain hands-on experience in designing and analyzing complex electrical systems.

Program Highlights

  • Power Systems: Generation and distribution of electricity.
  • Analog/Digital Circuits: Fundamental circuit design.
  • Telecommunications: Communication technologies.
  • Smart Grids: Modern power management.
