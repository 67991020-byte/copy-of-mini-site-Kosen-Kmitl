Diploma of Engineering in Computer Engineering

A 5-year program dedicated to computer hardware, software, and network systems. The curriculum covers everything from embedded systems to advanced software development and AI.

Program Highlights

  • Embedded Systems: Hardware-software integration.
  • IoT (Internet of Things): Connecting devices to the cloud.
  • AI & Data Science: Machine learning and data analysis.
  • Network Security: Protecting digital infrastructure.
