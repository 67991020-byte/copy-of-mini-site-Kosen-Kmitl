Bachelor of Engineering

Advanced Innovation Engineering

A 2-year continuing program designed for graduates with an Associate Degree. This program emphasizes advanced research, industrial collaboration, and practical innovation management.

Key Features

  • Research-Driven: Focus on solving real-world industrial problems through research.
  • Industrial Collaboration: Work closely with leading partners in the EEC (Eastern Economic Corridor).
  • Innovation Management: Learn to manage and commercialize engineering innovations.
  • Scholarships: Opportunities for financial support and internships.
